Tavern Legends

What Awaits You

01
Living Medieval Town

A fully realized 14th-century town with dynamic weather, a real day-night cycle, cozy village squares and your own home. The world reacts to the time of day and season.

02
Realistic Barkeeping

Tap drinks, cook meals, sweep floors and wipe tables. Fast service earns tips and loyalty. Let guests wait too long and word spreads fast.

03
7 Customer Classes

Beggars, peasants, guards, nobles, mages, princes and kings — each with unique needs, budgets and social effects. A king’s visit can make or break your reputation.

04
Staff Management

Hire up to four workers per tavern: cooks, waiters and cleaners. Review applications, set priorities and manage wages to build a team that runs itself.

05
Custom Building

Design your layout from scratch. Place seating, bars, kitchens and storage. Decorate with candles, carpets and table settings to improve your tavern’s prestige rating.

06
Recipes & Brewing

Craft your own menu with a wide selection of ingredients and spices. Brew your own craft beer in the cellar. Quality and variety unlock increasingly prestigious clientele.

07
Dynamic Weather

Rain drives people indoors. Sunshine attracts strollers. Thunderstorms change the atmosphere entirely. The world around your tavern is alive and reacts to every condition.

08
NPC Interaction

Speak with the townspeople — the blacksmith, market traders, neighbours. Build relationships with the community that surrounds your tavern.

09
Twitch Integration

Let your stream audience participate directly in your tavern run. A first-class feature for content creators who want their community in the thick of it.

CROWFORGE Games is an independent one-person studio based in Austria, founded in 2024. We build deeply immersive games set in historical worlds — crafted with precision, simulated with care, and shaped directly by the players who believe in them.

Our debut title, Tavern Legends, entered Steam Early Access in July 2025 and has earned Very Positive reviews from its growing community. Our second game, Werewolf Hunter: Night of Horror, is currently in active development.

As a solo operation, every update is hand-built and every piece of feedback is read personally. Your bug reports, feature ideas and community discussions directly determine what gets built next.
